❓ Help
What is this?

70 phase-stepped electronics visualizers. Each shows code or physics executing one phase at a time.

Where do I start?

The ⭐ Top 5. Ohm\'s Water Pipe is the universal foundation.

For what age?

Works 8–14 with adult help, 14+ solo. Each viz is self-contained.

Is my data private?

Yes. All runs locally. No tracking.
